what's a digital forest?

A digital forest is a site design I've developed to share my work on vernacular systems design. I wanted the form to reflect my process of articulating interrelated ideas simultaneously and continuously. Digital gardening, a form of knowledge sharing for publicly "tending to" ideas, resonated with this intention. I've added to it a visual archive of the iterative process, using tree-ring links to record and navigate how an article has grown over time. The tree rings were inspired by the icons denoting ideation stage on Maggie Appleton's digital garden.
