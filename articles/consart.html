The discrepancy between majority will (to end suffering under capitalism) and the unchanging reality (of majority suffering under capitalism) is dually confounding and logical. Capitalism is both the source of will, and the reason will is not materialized.//It mechanizes our reality, and the ways we concieve beyond it. (). I am interested in the ways information technology perpetrates, and can potentially narrow, this discrepancy b/w will and reality.
