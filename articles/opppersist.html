Why do dominant oppressive systems persist despite majority opposition?

    Ring#: 1
    Ring-date: Thurs March 7, 2024

Dominant oppressive systems persist because they were designed to. These systems are not broken.

One reason of interest is that it it takes more energy to embody positive than negative values due to differing value of labour

The reasons of focus however relate to the broken systems idea. I am invested in the counter to fixing systems, and instead learning how to capacitate autonomous systems design, organizing, adaptation and embodiment. The two problems here are:

a) collectives lack coherent consolidated awareness of same-intent initiatives, fragmentation and reiteration dilutes energy energy and thus efficacy

b) people lack agency to thoroughly articulate and actualize ideas and initiatives for systems that are autonomous from dominant oppressive systems

The solutions I am interested are clearly stated as the inversion of the latter problems. I intend to design and research ways that digital interfaces can enable the inverted solutions of the latter problems. Ideally their aggregated affect, specifically gained autonomy, will capacitate divestment from dominant oppressive systems, alluded to in the first problem listed.

are these solutions inherently and inevitably contradicting? can you successfully, simultaneously enable coherent consolidation of informed initiative aND increased agency for autonomous systems design?
