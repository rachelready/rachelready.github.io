how are systems translated spatially?

    Ring#: 1

Is there a seperation between system architecture and its physical realization? what would be the use of such a thing? In this case information architecture is intended to make tangible/visible intentionally embodied systems that are not otherwise supported or reflected by the built environment. The embodied systems also likely exist in digital space, at least partially, so an interface can seamlessly represent those facades. This spatial translatation of vernacular systems, has potential to facilitate the sustenenace of such systems, including emergent adaptation and scaling.

How are intended embodiments translated into space? in the built environment? in a digital interface?
